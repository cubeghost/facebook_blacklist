Blacklist for Facebook

Hide the posts you don't want to see—before you even see them.

Blacklist for Facebook allows you to pre-emptively hide posts with certain words in them. It's that easy.

Get it now in the Chrome Web Store.

Blacklist is still fresh out of development- if you run into any bugs, go ahead and email me at alex@bldwn.co, or send me a tweet at @cubeghost.

Instructions

When you install Blacklist, refresh Facebook- you'll find the options under the right-hand sidebar on the home page. Add the words you want to blacklist as a comma-separated list.

You can also choose to hide the Trending stories in your sidebar and/or only blacklist posts which also contain a "cw" or "tw". [1]

Extensions for Safari and Firefox are coming soon.

1. These stand for "content warning" and "trigger warning", respectively. Some Facebook groups use these, but if your friends don't, don't worry about it, just keep it unchecked.
